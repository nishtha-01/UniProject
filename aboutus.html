ABOUT US



Learning Space is a Computer Science portal for students and working professionals. It aims to provide computer science content to budding coders. It also has the feature of an Entrepreneurship cell where a student can post their ideas or articles and other students and companies can view it and can even promote the idea.


We developed this website, Learning Space precisely to target the college students to provide them a platform actually a single platform for multiple tasks, like studying, posting or writing articles, asking queries, solving challenges posted by companies. The specific feature we made of this website is the Entrepreneur cell where a student can post their ideas or articles and other students and companies can view it and can even promote the idea. This will definitely be developing a spirit of entrepreneurship among the students, and not only this but can even provide solutions to currently existing problems in the surrounding.
